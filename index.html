What/Who is a Highly Sensible Person (HSP)?A metaphoric soundscape to experience a Highly Sensitive Person’s hearing 15-20% of the population perceive and feel differently. They are more aware of subtleties but are also more easily overwhelmed. They are called Highly Sensitive Persons. High Sensitivity is a personality trait that is not to be confused with extro-/introversion. Furthermore, every HSP is different. This project focuses on a very small part of High Sensitivity, the perception of hearing. HSPs have a lower threshold of hearing but are also more easily overwhelmed by intense, and loud sounds. This project tries to provide a metaphor for a Highly Sensitive Person’s hearing. This metaphor enables non-HSPs to experience a HSP’s hearing.
